LEO CLUB BIT


The Worldwide Association of Lions Clubs, often known as Lions Clubs International, is an international non-political service organisation formed in Chicago, Illinois, by Melvin Jones in 1916. The headquarters of the corporation are currently located in Oak Brook, Illinois. On August 1, 2014, the BIT LEO club was created with the purpose of helping society by organising activities with the support of student volunteers and building leadership characteristics. Our organisation has organised a number of public awareness campaigns, community service projects, and leadership development programmes since then. In addition, we concentrate on health-care development, elder care, children, disabled people, literacy and education, and self-development.
